Hannah English Course

The Hannah English Course is not just an English course, but an innovative missionary tool. Through it, students learn English while immersing themselves in the Word of God, becoming fluent in the language and strengthened in their faith. Developed to support English teaching in our Learning Centers and to facilitate the expansion of the project to new mission hubs across Southeast Asia.

Grammar: Mastering English in Context

Grammar lessons are presented in a contextualized and practical manner, integrating with vocabulary and stories to help students understand and apply grammatical rules effectively.

Mixed Letters: Strengthen Word Structure

Mixed Letters strengthens memory and word structure in an interactive way, helping learners internalize correct spelling.

Memory Game: Enhance Vocabulary Through Play

The Memory Game combines fun and learning, helping students reinforce vocabulary and word memorization through an interactive matching game format.

Word Search: Fun with Vocabulary Puzzles

Word Search activities enhance vocabulary learning by challenging students to find and recognize words in a grid, improving spelling and visual word recognition.

Speak Cards: Practice Pronunciation Interactively

Speak Cards show images and words that can be played aloud. Students pronounce the word, and the app evaluates pronunciation accuracy.

Quizzes: Interactive Question Games

A question game focused on biblical, linguistic, and grammatical content from each lesson, with immediate feedback on correct and incorrect answers.

HannahVideos: Dynamic Video Learning

HannahVideos are central to the course, tailored for each unit to reflect biblical themes, grammatical content, and vocabulary. Engaging videos help reinforce learning through visual and auditory means.

Mascot Hannah: Your Learning Companion

Hannah guides learners through each lesson, making learning engaging, relatable, and fun for children, youth, and adults.

Robot Samuel: AI as Your Ally

What it is: An AI assistant that monitors activity difficulty, provides real-time tips, and adapts to each student's level.

Why it's amazing: Enables natural conversations with the student, calling them by name and using the topic of the recently studied lesson. It corrects grammar and pronunciation in a friendly and motivating way, boosting confidence when speaking English.
