Hannah English Course

The Hannah English Course is not just an English course, but an innovative missionary tool. Through it, students learn English while immersing themselves in the Word of God, becoming fluent in the language and strengthened in their faith. Developed to support English teaching in our Learning Centers and to facilitate the expansion of the project to new mission hubs across Southeast Asia.

Grammar: Mastering English in Context

Grammar lessons are presented in a contextualized and practical manner, integrating with vocabulary and stories to help students apply rules effectively.

Mixed Letters: Strengthen Word Structure

Arrange mixed letters to internalize spelling patterns and reinforce memory.

Memory Game: Enhance Vocabulary Through Play

Match cards in a fun format to reinforce and recall vocabulary effectively.

Word Search: Fun with Vocabulary Puzzles

Find hidden words in a grid to improve spelling and visual recognition.

Speak Cards: Practice Pronunciation Interactively

Listen to words and images, then speak and receive instant AI feedback on pronunciation.

Quizzes: Interactive Question Games

Test knowledge with engaging question sets and immediate feedback on answers.

StoryCards: Learn with Biblical Stories

Delve into Bible narratives crafted to teach specific grammar and vocabulary topics.

Flashcards: Interactive Vocabulary Building

Reinforce key words with image-word pairs that boost memorization and recall.

Biblical Lessons: Connect Bible Lessons to Life

Apply spiritual teachings in matching games and life lesson activities for deeper understanding.

Tests: Assess Your Progress

Evaluate your understanding with regular assessments designed to guide further learning.

HannahVideos: Dynamic Video Learning

Engaging videos tailored to biblical themes reinforce learning visually and audibly.

Mascot Hannah: Your Learning Companion

Hannah guides learners through lessons, making each session relatable and fun.

Robot Samuel: AI as Your Ally

What it is: An AI assistant that monitors difficulty, offers tips, and adapts to each student.

Why it's amazing: Natural conversations that correct grammar and pronunciation in real-time.

Real Impact in Our Classrooms

See students grow in language skills and faith through hands-on use in our Learning Centers.
