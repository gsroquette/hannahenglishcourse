StoryCards: Learn English with Biblical Stories

Flashcards: Interactive Vocabulary Building

Flashcards reinforce vocabulary learning through dynamic and visual means. Each card displays an image on one side and the corresponding word on the other, facilitating easy memorization and association.

Robot Samuel: AI as Your Ally

What it is: An AI assistant that monitors activity difficulty, provides real-time tips, and adapts to each student's level.

Why it's amazing: Enables natural conversations with the student, calling them by name and using the topic of the recently studied lesson. It corrects grammar and pronunciation in a friendly and motivating way, boosting confidence when speaking English.

Speak Cards: Practice Pronunciation Interactively

Speak Cards are cards that show images and words that can be heard by the student. The student then pronounces the word from the card, and the app evaluates whether the pronunciation is correct or incorrect.
