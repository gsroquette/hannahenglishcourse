Imagine an app that teaches English in a fun and interactive way, perfectly aligned with the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages), while also sharing Christian values and deepening biblical knowledge.

This is the Hannah English Course—a gamified program featuring Bible stories, music, videos, and the powerful AI assistance of “Samuel the Robot,” ensuring each lesson is unique and captivating.

Our protagonist, Hannah, guides children, youth, and even adults through a linguistic adventure that goes far beyond grammar and vocabulary: each Bible passage and lesson becomes a life-changing encounter with God’s Word.

Why Hannah Learning Center Is Unparalleled

  • Dynamic and Fun Learning: Cutting-edge methods combine technology and creative teaching tools, making each lesson an exciting discovery.
  • Faithful to the Gospel: Carefully chosen Bible stories reinforce Christian principles, making language learning relevant and transformative.
  • Engaging Gamification: Earn points, complete challenges, receive certificates, and enjoy interactive activities that motivate continuous progress.
  • Suitable for All Ages: Perfect for both children and adults seeking to improve their English on a solid foundation.

At the Hannah Learning Center, learning English goes far beyond grammar and vocabulary drills. Each verse, each story, and every activity enriches the mind and strengthens faith.

How This Impacts the Kampot Community

Quality Education: We provide a unique opportunity for local Christians to learn English with modern, effective tools.

Evangelism: We use English classes as a bridge to share the Gospel, presenting Bible stories that can transform lives.

Building a Stronger Church: Local church members and community leaders will be actively involved, fostering unity and spiritual growth.

Mission Expansion: Beyond education, this project will help expand Christian ministry, solidifying the Christian presence in Kampot and beyond.

By God’s grace, Hannah Foundation has been developing Learning Centers in Southeast Asia for five years, with established centers in Thailand and Myanmar. Now, we are thrilled to announce our expansion to Kampot, Cambodia, thanks to the facilities provided by the local Methodist Church.

Pilot Project – How We Begin

Our plan is to start with two English classes, offering lessons twice a week. As the program takes root, we aim to increase the number of students and classes, and eventually introduce other courses as needed. Once we form a group of students with basic English knowledge, our goal is to expand the project to establish a Christian school with a complete curriculum covering all subjects, with God's help.

Besides teaching English, the course promotes Christian values and helps shape responsible citizens—connected to God’s Word and committed to society.

Meet Gustavo Roquette

Gustavo Roquette is a Brazilian missionary and the creator of the Hannah English Course, an innovative app that blends English learning with biblical teachings. This initiative highlights his dedication to using education as a tool to fulfill God's mission.

With over 20 years of experience in education and more than seven years in cross-cultural missions in Thailand, Gustavo holds degrees in Theology from the César Dacorso Filho Methodist Seminary (Brazil), Intercultural Studies from the Malaysia Baptist Theological Seminary, and Educational Technology from the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro. His expertise includes roles as an educator, school director, and teacher trainer for Brazil’s Ministry of Education, as well as planting churches in Brazil, Thailand, and Myanmar.

In Thailand, Gustavo and his wife Carla founded the Hannah Foundation and lead the HIS Learning Center together. This center combines quality education with Christian values, making a positive impact on local communities.
