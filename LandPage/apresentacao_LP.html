App Features

Home Screen: Your Learning Hub

Access lessons, games, and resources from a clean, intuitive dashboard designed for all ages.

Learning Board: Track Your Journey

Visualize progress through gamified levels and unlock new activities as you advance.

StoryCards: Learn English with Biblical Stories

StoryCards teach English meaningfully by using biblical stories that connect grammar, vocabulary and comprehension. Each story covers specific language targets, making learning engaging and significant.

Flashcards: Interactive Vocabulary Building

Flashcards reinforce vocabulary learning visually. Each card displays an image on one side and the corresponding word on the other, facilitating easy memorization and association.

Quizzes: Interactive Question Games

A question game focused on biblical, linguistic and grammatical content from each lesson, with immediate feedback on correct and incorrect answers.

Memory Game: Enhance Vocabulary Through Play

Match cards to reinforce and recall vocabulary effectively.

Word Search: Fun with Vocabulary Puzzles

Word Search activities enhance vocabulary learning by challenging students to find and recognize words in a grid, improving spelling and visual word recognition in an enjoyable way.

Spelling Tools: Practice Accurate Writing

Missing Words and Mixed Letters improve spelling and vocabulary. Missing Words focuses on word recognition and spelling through image association, while Mixed Letters strengthens memory and word structure interactively.

Grammar: Mastering English in Context

Grammar lessons are presented in a contextualized and practical manner, integrating with vocabulary and stories to help students understand and apply grammatical rules effectively.

Speak Cards: Practice Pronunciation Interactively

Listen to image-word cards, then pronounce them – the app evaluates accuracy and offers feedback to perfect pronunciation.

HannahVideos: Dynamic Video Learning

Engaging videos tailored to each unit reinforce biblical themes, grammar and vocabulary through visual and auditory learning.

Exploring Biblical Lessons: Connect Bible Lessons to Life

Connect biblical lessons to real-life situations through Matching Games and Life Lessons activities, fostering reflection and practical application of the values learned in both linguistic and spiritual contexts.

Tests: Assess Your Progress

Regular tests are essential tools for evaluating understanding and mastery of the material, tracking progress and identifying areas for improvement.

Robot Samuel: AI as Your Ally

What it is: An AI assistant that monitors activity difficulty, provides real-time tips and adapts to each student’s level.

Why it’s amazing: Enables natural conversations, calls students by name and uses the current lesson topic. Corrects grammar and pronunciation in a friendly, motivating way, boosting confidence.
