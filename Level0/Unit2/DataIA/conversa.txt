TITLE: How Old Are You?

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):
1.Count from 1 to 5
2.Count from 6 to 10
3. Understand the question "How old are you?"
4. Answer "I am ([number]) years old."

Flow (example):
AI: Hello! Do you know how to count?
Student: Yes!
AI: Great! Count 1 to 5.
Student: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5.
AI: Nice job! Now count 6 to 10.
Student: 6, 7, 8, 9, 10.
AI: Wow! You can count to 10! How old are you?
Student: I am seven years old.
AI: Awesome work, ([Student’s Name])! See you next time!
