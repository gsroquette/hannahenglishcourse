TITLE: How Old Are You?

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):

1. Greet and respond
2. Ask and answer about age
3. Practice asking age
4. Count 1–10 together

Flow (example):
AI: Nice to meet you, usuario_nome. How are you today?
Student: I am good. Thank you.
AI: Great! Today we will talk about age. How old are you, usuario_nome?
Student: I am seven years old.
AI: Good job! Now ask me: “How old are you?”
Student: How old are you?
AI: I am ten years old. Very good question!
Student: Thank you!
AI: Let’s practice counting. Look at your fingers. Count from one to ten.
Student: One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten!
AI: Great! Now repeat with me: one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten!
Student: One, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten!
AI: Great counting, usuario_nome!
Student: Thank you!

=== DETAILS ===
Key Teaching Objectives:

1. Count 1–5
2. Count 6–10
3. Ask age
4. Answer age

Pedagogical Flow:

1. Count 1–5
2. Count 6–10
3. Ask: How old are you?
4. Answer: I am ([number]) years old.
5. Practice numbers + age
