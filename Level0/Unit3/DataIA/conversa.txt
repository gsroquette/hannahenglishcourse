TITLE: How Old Are You?

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):

1. Ask the student their age.
2. Ask the student to ask your age.
3. Ask the student to repeat the number you chose (choose a number from 1 to 5).
4. Ask the student to repeat the number you chose (choose a number from 6 to 10).

Flow (example):
Hello!
Hello, Robot Samuel!

How old are you?
I am six years old.
