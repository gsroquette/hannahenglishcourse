#title: José e a túnica colorida Story Cards (Gn 37:3; Gn 41:42)

#imagem1
"Joseph lives in Canaan with his family. God has a plan for him."

#imagem2
"His father gives him a special coat with red, blue, and yellow colors."

#imagem3
"Joseph’s brothers see the bright coat. Their faces turn red with anger."

#imagem4
"The brothers throw Joseph into a big black circle hole. Joseph prays to God."

#imagem5
"Joseph is sold to travelers. His colorful coat is gone. He is sad."

#imagem6
"In Egypt, Joseph sees big triangle pyramids. He works hard and trusts God."

#imagem7
"The king sees Joseph is wise. He gives Joseph a white coat and a gold circle ring."

#imagem8
"Joseph becomes a leader in Egypt. He stores food in big square places. Many people come to Egypt for food."

#imagem9
"Joseph forgives his brothers. He says, 'God has a good plan.' His heart is bright like yellow light."

#imagem10
"God has a plan for you, too. Trust in Him. His love is like a colorful rainbow."
