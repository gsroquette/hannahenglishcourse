TITLE: How Old Are You?

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):

1. Ask the student their age.
2. Ask the student to ask your age.
3. (choose a number from 1 to 5) Ask the student to repeat the number you chose.
4. (choose a number from 6 to 10) Ask the student to repeat the number you chose.

Flow (example):
Hello!
Hello, Robot Samuel!

How old are you?
I am six years old.
