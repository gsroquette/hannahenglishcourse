Title: A / AN — God is Good, God is the Creator

# GRAMMAR
God is good. God is the creator. Look at the world! We see many things. We use **a** and **an** to talk about one thing.

## 1. One Thing: A / AN
#IMG:imagem1.png
We use **a** or **an** before one thing.

Say:
a tree  
a rabbit  
an apple  
an elephant  

TableStart
a | an
a tree | an apple
a rabbit | an elephant
a person | an orange
TableEnd

## 2. A
#IMG:imagem2.png
We say **a**:

Say:
a tree  
a rabbit  
a person  

TableStart
Word | Sentence
tree | This is a tree.
rabbit | This is a rabbit.
person | This is a person.
TableEnd

## 3. AN
#IMG:imagem3.png
We say **an** when the word starts with A, E, I, O, U.

Say:
an apple  
an elephant  
an orange  

TableStart
Word | Sentence
apple | This is an apple.
elephant | This is an elephant.
orange | This is an orange.
TableEnd
