TITLE: What is in your bag? A or An

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):

1. Ask the student: "What is in your bag?"
2. The student answers with "a" or "an" (e.g., "a book", "a pencil"). Give correct examples if needed.
3. Ask again: "What is in your bag?" to get more answers.
4. End the conversation after the student uses "a" at least once and "an" at least once correctly.

Flow (example):

Student: A book.
