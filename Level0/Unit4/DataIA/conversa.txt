TITLE: What is this?

PINNED_BRIEF:
Goals (in this order):

1. Understand "What is this?"
2. Answer with "a" or "an"
3. Name simple objects
4. Understand "What is in your bag?"
5. Answer about bag items

Flow (example):
AI: Hello, ([Student’s Name])! What is this?
Student: This is a cat.
AI: Good! What is this?
Student: This is an apple.
AI: Great! What is in your bag?
Student: A book.
AI: Nice job, ([Student’s Name])! You did great!

=== DETAILS ===
Key Teaching Objectives:

1. Recognize simple objects
2. Use "a" and "an" correctly
3. Answer "What is this?"
4. Answer "What is in your bag?"

Pedagogical Flow:

1. Greet student
2. Ask "What is this?"
3. Practice "a" and "an"
4. Ask about bag
5. Close with praise
