title|en-US|Creation Explorer — A / AN Adventure

subtitle|en-US|Objective
text|en-US|Practice *a* and *an* while exploring God’s creation.
text|en-US|Use the patterns: **What is this?** and **This is a / an _____.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Materials
text|en-US|Prepare picture cards (or simple objects) and place them face down:
text|en-US|- 🌳 tree
text|en-US|- 🐇 rabbit
text|en-US|- 🍎 apple
text|en-US|- 🐘 elephant
text|en-US|- 🍊 orange
text|en-US|- 👤 person

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|How to Play
text|en-US|1) Pick a card.
text|en-US|A student takes one card and shows it to the class.
text|en-US|The student asks: **What is this?**

text|en-US|2) Answer with a full sentence.
text|en-US|Another student answers: **This is a / an _____.**
text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|- **This is a tree.**
text|en-US|- **This is a rabbit.**
text|en-US|- **This is an apple.**
text|en-US|- **This is an elephant.**
text|en-US|- **This is an orange.**
text|en-US|- **This is a person.**

text|en-US|3) Score
text|en-US|- 1 point: correct *a / an*
text|en-US|- 2 points: correct *a / an* + full sentence

text|en-US|4) Continue
text|en-US|Play until all cards are used.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Challenge Round
text|en-US|Place **three** cards together.
text|en-US|The student must say **three** full sentences.
text|en-US|Example (🍎 🐘 🌳):
text|en-US|- **This is an apple.**
text|en-US|- **This is an elephant.**
text|en-US|- **This is a tree.**
text|en-US|Bonus: +3 points if all three sentences are correct.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Creation Walk (Fun Variant)
text|en-US|Spread the pictures around the room.
text|en-US|Students walk and stop at a picture.
text|en-US|They say:
text|en-US|**Look! What is this?**
text|en-US|**This is a / an _____.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Reflection
text|en-US|Ask and answer:
text|en-US|- **Who created the world?** → **God created the world.**
text|en-US|- **Is God good?** → **God is good.**
text|en-US|- **What did God create?** → **God created trees, animals, and people.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Optional Task
text|en-US|Draw one thing from God’s creation.
text|en-US|Write one sentence:
text|en-US|**This is a / an _____.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Closing
text|en-US|Say together:
text|en-US|**God is good.**
text|en-US|**God is the creator.**
text|en-US|**God loves you.**
