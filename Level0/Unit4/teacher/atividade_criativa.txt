title|en-US|Mystery Cards & Magic Bag

subtitle|en-US|Objective
text|en-US|Practice **What is this?** and **What is in your bag?**
text|en-US|Use **a** and **an** to talk about one thing.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Materials
text|en-US|Prepare picture cards:
text|en-US|- tree
text|en-US|- rabbit
text|en-US|- apple
text|en-US|- elephant
text|en-US|- orange
text|en-US|- person
text|en-US|Prepare one **mystery bag** (a backpack or small bag).

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Game 1 — Mystery Card

text|en-US|1) Pick a card.
text|en-US|A student takes one card and hides it.

text|en-US|2) Show slowly.
text|en-US|The student shows the card little by little.

text|en-US|The class asks:
text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?

text|en-US|A student answers:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is a / an ______.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|**This is a tree.**
text|en-US|**This is a rabbit.**
text|en-US|**This is an apple.**
text|en-US|**This is an elephant.**
text|en-US|**This is an orange.**
text|en-US|**This is a person.**

text|en-US|If correct → the student keeps the card.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Game 2 — Magic Bag

text|en-US|Put some cards inside the bag.

text|en-US|One student closes their eyes and takes one card.

text|en-US|The class asks:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is in your bag?

text|en-US|The student looks and answers:

text-bold-large|en-US|In my bag, I have a / an ______.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|**In my bag, I have an apple.**
text|en-US|**In my bag, I have a rabbit.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Speed Round

text|en-US|Show cards very quickly.

text|en-US|Students must answer fast:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is a tree!
text-bold-large|en-US|This is an apple!
text-bold-large|en-US|This is an elephant!

text|en-US|Fast and correct answers score points.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Action Round

text|en-US|Students act like the object.

text|en-US|Example:
text|en-US|A student acts like an elephant.

text|en-US|The class asks:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?

text|en-US|Students answer:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is an elephant!

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Mini Challenge

text|en-US|Students make one sentence about their bag.

text-bold-large|en-US|In my bag, I have a ______.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|**In my bag, I have a book.**
text|en-US|**In my bag, I have an apple.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Finish

text|en-US|Say together:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?
text-bold-large|en-US|This is a / an ______.

text-bold-large|en-US|What is in your bag?
text-bold-large|en-US|In my bag, I have a / an ______.
