title|en-US|God’s Good World

subtitle|en-US|Objective
text|en-US|Help students understand three simple truths:
text|en-US|**God is the creator.**
text|en-US|**Everything God makes is good.**
text|en-US|**God loves people.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Materials
text|en-US|Prepare picture cards or drawings:
text|en-US|- light
text|en-US|- sky
text|en-US|- water
text|en-US|- land
text|en-US|- trees
text|en-US|- sun
text|en-US|- moon
text|en-US|- stars
text|en-US|- animals
text|en-US|- people
text|en-US|Prepare one large paper or poster for the class.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Step 1 — Look at the World

text|en-US|The teacher shows a picture.

text-bold-large|en-US|Look!

text|en-US|The teacher asks:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?

text|en-US|Students answer:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is the ______.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|**This is the sun.**
text|en-US|**This is the sky.**
text|en-US|**This is the water.**
text|en-US|**This is the land.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Step 2 — Build the World

text|en-US|Put a large paper on the board.

text-bold-large|en-US|The World

text|en-US|Students come to the front and place pictures on the poster.

text|en-US|Each student says:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is the ______.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|**This is the sun.**
text|en-US|**This is the moon.**
text|en-US|**This is the water.**
text|en-US|**This is the land.**
text|en-US|**These are trees.**

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Step 3 — God Is Good

text|en-US|When the poster is ready, the teacher points to the world.

text|en-US|The teacher says:

text-bold-large|en-US|God is the creator.

text|en-US|Students repeat:

text-bold-large|en-US|God is the creator.

text|en-US|The teacher says:

text-bold-large|en-US|God is good.

text|en-US|Students repeat:

text-bold-large|en-US|God is good.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Step 4 — People

text|en-US|The teacher shows the picture of people.

text|en-US|The teacher asks:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?

text|en-US|Students answer:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is a person.

text|en-US|The teacher says:

text-bold-large|en-US|God loves people.

text|en-US|Students repeat:

text-bold-large|en-US|God loves people.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Creative Moment

text|en-US|Students draw something from the world.

text|en-US|Examples:
text|en-US|- the sun
text|en-US|- the sky
text|en-US|- trees
text|en-US|- animals
text|en-US|- people

text|en-US|Students color their drawings.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Show and Share

text|en-US|Students show their drawing.

text|en-US|The class asks:

text-bold-large|en-US|What is this?

text|en-US|The student answers:

text-bold-large|en-US|This is the ______.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|en-US|Closing Message

text-bold-large|en-US|God is the creator.
text-bold-large|en-US|God is good.
text-bold-large|en-US|God loves you.
