Title: God Knows My Name

# GRAMMAR
God knows Moses. God knows you too!  
We can say who we are in English.

## 1. I am...
#IMG:imagem1.png
We can say who we are with **I am**.

TableStart
I am | Example
I am Moses | I am a boy
I am happy | I am a girl
TableEnd

## 2. He is...
#IMG:imagem2.png
We can talk about a boy or a man with **He is**.

TableStart
He is | Example
He is Moses | He is a shepherd
He is happy | He is on the mountain
TableEnd

## 3. She is...
#IMG:imagem3.png
We can talk about a girl or a woman with **She is**.

TableStart
She is | Example
She is a girl | She is happy
She is my mom | She is kind
TableEnd

## 4. God knows you
#IMG:imagem4.png
God knows your name.  
You are special!

TableStart
Sentence | Meaning
I am special | God loves me
He is special | God loves him
She is special | God loves her
TableEnd
