#TITLE: IS/ARE/AM Questions - Creation Story Cards (Genesis 1-2)

Exercise 1:
Rewrite the sentences using the correct short form.

a. She is happy. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: She’s] happy. 
(*She’s happy.*)
b. They are learning about God’s creation. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: They’re] learning about God’s creation.
c. It is a beautiful world. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: It’s] a beautiful world.
d. That is a bright sun. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: That’s] a bright sun.
e. I am amazed by God’s work. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: I’m] amazed by God’s work.
f. You are a wonderful creation of God. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: You’re] a wonderful creation of God.

Exercise 2:
Fill in the blanks with **am, is, or are**.

a. The world ___ beautiful because God made it. [size:medium] [Answer: is] 
(*The world is beautiful because God made it.*)
b. I ___ thankful for God’s love. [size:medium] [Answer: am]
c. The stars ___ bright in the night sky. [size:medium] [Answer: are]
d. The land and sea ___ full of life. [size:medium] [Answer: are]
e. The sun ___ shining brightly. [size:medium] [Answer: is]
f. My friends and I ___ learning about creation. [size:medium] [Answer: are]
g. Adam and Eve ___ in God’s garden. [size:medium] [Answer: are]
h. I ___ happy because God loves me. [size:medium] [Answer: am]

Exercise 3:
Complete the sentences with the correct word.

a. Steve is ill. ___ in bed. [size:medium] [Answer: He’s] 
(*He’s in bed.*)
b. I’m not hungry, but I ___ thirsty. [size:medium] [Answer: am]
c. Mr. Thomas is a very old man. He is ___ years old. [size:medium] [Answer: 98]
d. These trees aren’t small, but they ___ big and beautiful. [size:medium] [Answer: are]
e. The weather is nice today. It ___ [size:medium] [Answer: is] warm and sunny.
f. ‘___ [size:medium] [Answer: Are] you late?’ ‘No, I’m not. I’m early!’
g. Catherine isn’t at home. She ___ [size:medium] [Answer: is] at work.
h. ‘___ [size:medium] [Answer: Is] this your coat?’ ‘Oh, thank you very much!’
