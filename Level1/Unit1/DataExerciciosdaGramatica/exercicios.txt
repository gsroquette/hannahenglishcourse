#TITLE: IS/ARE/AM Questions - Creation Story Cards (Genesis 1-2)

Exercise 1:
Rewrite the sentences using the correct short form.

1. She is happy. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: She’s] happy.
2. They are learning about God’s creation. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: They’re] learning about God’s creation.
3. It is a beautiful world. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: It’s] a beautiful world.
4. That is a bright sun. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: That’s] a bright sun.
5. I am amazed by God’s work. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: I’m] amazed by God’s work.
6. You are a wonderful creation of God. → ___ [size:medium] [Answer: You’re] a wonderful creation of God.

3. The stars ___ [size:medium] [Answer: are] bright in the night sky.
4. The land and sea ___ [size:medium] [Answer: are] full of life.
5. The sun ___ [size:medium] [Answer: is] shining brightly.
6. My friends and I ___ [size:medium] [Answer: are] learning about creation.
7. Adam and Eve ___ [size:medium] [Answer: are] in God’s garden.

Exercise 3:
Complete the sentences with the correct word.

1. Steve is ill. ___ [size:medium] [Answer: He’s] in bed.
2. I’m not hungry, but I ___ [size:medium] [Answer: am] thirsty.
3. Mr. Thomas is a very old man. He is ___ [size:medium] [Answer: 98].
4. These trees aren’t small, but they ___ [size:medium] [Answer: are] big and beautiful.
5. The weather is nice today. It ___ [size:medium] [Answer: is] warm and sunny.
6. ‘___ [size:medium] [Answer: Are] you late?’ ‘No, I’m not. I’m early!’
7. Catherine isn’t at home. She ___ [size:medium] [Answer: is] at work.
8. ‘___ [size:medium] [Answer: Is] this your coat?’ ‘Oh, thank you very much!’
