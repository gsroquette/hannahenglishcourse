lesson|God is Powerful and the Creator|God made everything.|God created the heavens and the earth, light, sky, sea, plants, and animals. This shows us His power and His love for us. (Genesis 1:1-25)
lesson|God Loves Us|God made everything good.|After God created everything, He saw that it was good. This shows us His love for all of creation, including us. (Genesis 1:31)
lesson|Everything Has a Purpose|God made everything with purpose.|God created everything with order and purpose, showing that our lives also have meaning. (Genesis 1:14-26)
lesson|Appreciate Nature|God made beautiful nature.|God's creation is beautiful, and we should take care of it because it is His gift to us. (Genesis 1:11-18)
lesson|Rest is Important|God rested on the seventh day.|God rested on the seventh day to show us the importance of rest and reflection. (Genesis 1:31, Genesis 2:2-3)
lesson|We Are Special|God made us special.|God created humans in His image, making us unique and special to Him. (Genesis 1:27)
lesson|Be Thankful|God was happy with His creation.|God looked at all He made and said it was very good. This reminds us to be thankful for the blessings around us. (Genesis 1:31)
