# EXERCISES: The Story of Adam and Eve (Genesis 3)

Exercise 1: Find the correct answers for the questions.

(A) The serpent.  
(B) Yes, they were afraid.  
(C) No, they were not.  
(D) The Tree of Knowledge.  
(E) Leaves and branches.  
(F) To hide from God.  
(G) Adam and Eve.  
(H) No, they didn’t obey God.  

1. Who spoke to Eve? ___[Answer: A]  
2. Were Adam and Eve afraid after eating the fruit? ___[Answer: B]  
3. Were they still happy after their disobedience? ___[Answer: C]  
4. What was in the middle of the garden? ___[Answer: D]  
5. What did Adam and Eve use to cover themselves? ___[Answer: E]  
6. Why did Adam and Eve hide? ___[Answer: F]  
7. Who ate the fruit first? ___[Answer: G]  
8. Did they obey God's command? ___[Answer: H]

Exercise 3: Complete the questions. Use **What / Who / Where / How**.

1. ___ speaks to Eve? [Answer: Who]  
2. ___ is the forbidden tree? [Answer: Where]  
3. ___ do Adam and Eve use to cover themselves? [Answer: What]  
4. ___ do they feel after eating the fruit? [Answer: How]  
5. ___ does God call in the garden? [Answer: Who]  
6. ___ does the serpent say to Eve? [Answer: What]  
7. ___ does God send Adam and Eve out of the garden? [Answer: Why]
