Exercise 3:
Complete the questions.

#BOX
**do, enjoy, go, like, start, teach, work**
#ENDBOX

1. What ___ ? [size:medium] [Answer: do you do]
   I live in God's creation.
2. How ___ about God's rules? [size:medium] [Answer: do you feel]
   They are important.
3. What time ___ in the morning? [size:medium] [Answer: do you pray]
   At sunrise.
4. How often ___ the Bible? [size:medium] [Answer: do you read]
   Every day.
5. How ___ to church? [size:medium] [Answer: do you go]
   Usually with my family.
6. And your friend. What ___ in? [size:medium] [Answer: does he believe]
   He believes in God.
7. What ___ us? [size:medium] [Answer: does Jesus teach]
   He teaches us love.
8. What ___ from us? [size:medium] [Answer: does God want]
   Obedience.
