#TITLE: Frequency Adverbs and Grammar – Cain and Abel Story Cards (Genesis 4)

Exercise 1:
Write sentences about Cain using the following frequency adverbs: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never.
a. Cain lived with his family. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain always lived with his family.]
b. Cain gave his best offering to God. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain never gave his best offering to God.]
c. Cain worked in the fields. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain often worked in the fields.]
d. Cain felt jealous of Abel. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain sometimes felt jealous of Abel.]
e. Cain thanked God for Abel’s gift. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain usually thanked God for Abel’s gift.]
f. Abel complained about God. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel never complained about God.]

Exercise 2:
Write these sentences with the adverb in parentheses.
a. Abel cares for the sheep. (usually) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel usually cares for the sheep.]
b. Cain drives his plow in the field. (often) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain often drives his plow in the field.]
c. God has spoken to Cain. (just) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God has just spoken to Cain.]
d. Cain chooses evil. (rarely) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain rarely chooses evil.]
e. God’s mercy fails. (never) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God’s mercy never fails.]
f. Cain admits his sin. (never) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain never admits his sin.]
g. Have you seen Abel pray? (ever) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Have you ever seen Abel pray?]
h. Does Cain still live in Nod? (still) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Does Cain still live in Nod?]
i. They offer their gifts to God. (always) ___ [size:large] [Answer: They always offer their gifts to God.]
j. Abel rests on the Sabbath. (usually) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel usually rests on the Sabbath.]
k. Is Cain already away from home? (already) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Is Cain already away from home?]
l. What do they choose as offerings? (usually) ___ [size:large] [Answer: What do they usually choose as offerings?]
m. God forgets our hearts. (never) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God never forgets our hearts.]
n. Abel thanks God for mercy. (often) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel often thanks God for mercy.]
o. Cain hides from God. (sometimes) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain sometimes hides from God.]

Exercise 3:
Write sentences with also.
a. Do you worship God? (praise Him) ___ [size:large] [Answer: I worship God and also praise Him.]
b. Does Abel care for the sheep? (offer his best) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel cares for the sheep and also offers his best.]
c. Did Cain leave his home? (wander in Nod) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain left his home and also wandered in Nod.]
d. Has God spoken to Cain? (warned him) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God has spoken to Cain and also warned him.]
e. Did Abel receive mercy? (see God’s justice) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Abel received mercy and also saw God’s justice.]

Exercise 4:
Write sentences with both and all.
a. Cain and Abel (give offerings) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain and Abel both gave offerings to God.]
b. Sheep and fruits (accepted) ___ [size:large] [Answer: Both the sheep and the fruits were accepted by God.]
c. Family members (worship God) ___ [size:large] [Answer: All family members worshiped God.]
d. Protection and mercy (God gives) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God gives both protection and mercy to Cain.]
e. Hearts and actions (God sees) ___ [size:large] [Answer: God sees all hearts and actions.]
