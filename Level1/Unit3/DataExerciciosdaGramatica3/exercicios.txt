Exercise 1:  
Correct the word order in the following sentences.

a. Cain often looks with jealousy at Abel. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain often looks at Abel with jealousy.]

c. Speaks to Cain God. ___ [size:large] [Answer: God speaks to Cain.]  
d. In the field Abel and Cain walk. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain and Abel walk in the field.]  
e. Cain his brother hurts in the field. ___ [size:large] [Answer: Cain hurts his brother in the field.]
