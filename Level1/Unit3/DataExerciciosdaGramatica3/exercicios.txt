Exercise 3:  
Complete the questions.  

Use these verbs:  

#BOX  
**do, enjoy, go, learn, play, start, teach, work**  
#ENDBOX  

1. What ___? [size:medium] [Answer: do you do]  
   I work as a teacher.  

2. How often ___ to church? [size:medium] [Answer: do you go]  
   Every Sunday.  

3. What time ___ your day? [size:medium] [Answer: do you start]  
   At 6 a.m.  

4. ___ your work? [size:medium] [Answer: Do you enjoy]  
   Yes, I love it.  

5. What ___ at school? [size:medium] [Answer: do you learn]  
   I learn English.  

6. What ___ to children? [size:medium] [Answer: does he teach]  
   He teaches Bible stories.  

7. Where ___? [size:medium] [Answer: do you work]  
   I work in a church.  

8. ___ football? [size:medium] [Answer: Do you play]  
   Yes, I do.
