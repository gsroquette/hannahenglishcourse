title|zh-CN|单词顺序 – 动词、宾语和频率副词 – 该隐与亚伯的故事

subtitle|zh-CN|介绍
text|zh-CN|在本课中，我们将学习如何在英语句子中正确地排列单词。我们会讲到谁做动作（主语）、动作是什么（动词）以及动作的接受者（宾语）。我们还会学习表示频率、时间和地点的词语该如何使用。

spacer|separator|

subtitle|zh-CN|什么是主语、动词和宾语？
text|zh-CN|每个英文句子都需要一个主语和动词。很多时候，还会有一个宾语。
text|zh-CN|主语是执行动作的人或事物。
text|zh-CN|动词是描述发生的动作。
text|zh-CN|宾语是接受这个动作的人或事物。

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]宾语      | [zh-CN]接受动作的对象   | [en-US]fruits

text-bold-large|en-US|Cain gives fruits.

table-row|mixed|[en-US]"fruits" | [zh-CN]是接受动作的对象（宾语）。

subtitle|zh-CN|1. 动词 + 宾语
text|zh-CN|在英语中，单词顺序非常重要。通常的结构是：
text-bold-large|zh-CN|主语 + 动词 + 宾语
text|zh-CN|动词和宾语通常放在一起，不应该分开。

text|zh-CN|看看故事中的例句：
text-bold-large|en-US|Abel gave his best sheep.
text-bold-large|en-US|Cain hurt his brother.
text-bold-large|en-US|God asked a question.

text|zh-CN|现在看看常见错误以及如何改正：

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]错误                                     | [zh-CN]正确

table-row|mixed|[en-US]Cain gave with love his best sheep.      | [en-US]Cain gave his best sheep with love.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]God asked with anger the question.       | [en-US]God asked the question with anger.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]He hurt quickly his brother.             | [en-US]He hurt his brother quickly.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|zh-CN|2. 频率副词
text|zh-CN|这些词表示动作发生的频率，比如：总是、经常、有时、从不……
text|zh-CN|频率副词通常放在实义动词前面，但在be动词（am/is/are）之后。

text|zh-CN|看看故事中的例句：
text-bold-large|en-US|Abel usually takes care of the sheep.
text-bold-large|en-US|Cain often works in the fields.
text-bold-large|en-US|They always lived with their family.
text-bold-large|en-US|Cain sometimes felt jealous.
text-bold-large|en-US|God never lies.

text|zh-CN|下面是更多例子：

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]副词      | [zh-CN]故事中的例句

table-row|mixed|[en-US]always    | [en-US]They always lived with their family.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]usually   | [en-US]Abel usually takes care of the sheep.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]often     | [en-US]Cain often works in the fields.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]sometimes | [en-US]Cain sometimes feels jealous.
table-row|mixed|[en-US]never     | [en-US]God never lies.

spacer|separator|

subtitle|zh-CN|3. 地点和时间
text|zh-CN|当我们使用表示“哪里”和“什么时候”的词语时，单词的顺序也很重要。
text|zh-CN|通常先说地点，然后说时间。

image|zh-CN|when-where.png

text|zh-CN|看看故事中的例句：
text-bold-large|en-US|Cain went to the field in the afternoon.
text-bold-large|en-US|Abel worked in the fields every day.
text-bold-large|en-US|God spoke to Cain that day.

text|zh-CN|现在看看一些常见错误和正确的表达方式：

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]错误                           | [zh-CN]正确

table-row|mixed|[en-US]Cain went yesterday to the field. | [en-US]Cain went to the field yesterday.

table-row|mixed|[en-US]Abel prayed every day at home.    | [en-US]Abel prayed at home every day.

table-row|mixed|[en-US]God spoke last night to Cain.     | [en-US]God spoke to Cain last night.
