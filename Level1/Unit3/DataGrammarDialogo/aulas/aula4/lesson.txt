subtitle|zh-CN|课程介绍
text|zh-CN|在本课中，我们将学习如何用英语谈论我们经常做的事情。
text|zh-CN|这是练习一般现在时和扩展词汇的好方法。

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]刷牙                 | [en-US]brush my teeth
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]祷告                 | [en-US]pray

text|zh-CN|我们可以使用一些频率副词来表达一个动作发生的频率，比如“总是”、“通常”、“有时”等。

table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]中文词语             | [en-US]Adverb
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]总是                 | [en-US]always
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]通常                 | [en-US]usually
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]经常                 | [en-US]often
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]有时                 | [en-US]sometimes
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]很少                 | [en-US]rarely
table-row|mixed|[zh-CN]从不                 | [en-US]never

spacer|separator|

subtitle|zh-CN|这些词怎么使用？
text|zh-CN|这些副词通常放在主要动词前，但在表示“是/在”的动词后面。
text|zh-CN|请看以下例句：

text-bold-large|en-US|I usually wake up at 7 o’clock.
text-bold-large|en-US|She always eats breakfast.
text-bold-large|en-US|We sometimes help our parents.
text-bold-large|en-US|They never go to school on Sunday.
text-bold-large|en-US|He is often tired in the morning.
