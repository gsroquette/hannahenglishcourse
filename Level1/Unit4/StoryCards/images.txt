#title: Joseph and His Brothers (Genesis 37-45)

#imagem1 
Joseph is a young boy. He has many brothers. They do not like him because he has a special coat.

#imagem2 
Joseph has a dream. In the dream, he is a leader. His brothers are not happy with the dream.

#imagem3 
His brothers plan to send Joseph away. They sell him to some people going to Egypt. Joseph is tied up and sad.

#imagem4 
Joseph works hard in Egypt. He becomes very important. He helps many people.

#imagem5 
There is no food in Joseph's home. His brothers go to Egypt to find food. They do not know Joseph is there.

#imagem6 
Joseph sees his brothers. They do not know him. He gives them food and tests their hearts.

#imagem7 
Joseph tells his brothers, "I am your brother!" They are surprised and happy.

#imagem8 
Joseph and his brothers go back to their father. They are together again.
