How it works

This guide is designed to help you make the most of the interactive resources available on our website, ensuring that our students learn English in an engaging, enjoyable, and participatory way. Below is a step-by-step guide on how to integrate each tool and activity into your lessons:

StoryCards: These cards are designed to introduce students to a new story, incorporating fresh vocabulary and concepts. Use them at the beginning of class to set the context for the story and prepare students for the activities that follow, while also reinforcing listening and reading skills.

Flashcards: Flashcards are useful for drilling vocabulary and helping students memorize key terms. Use them as a warm-up or review activity.

MemoryGame: This game reinforces vocabulary and concepts in a fun and interactive way. Students match pairs of cards to improve their memory and recall.

WordSearch: The word search activity challenges students to find vocabulary words within a grid. It's a great way to reinforce spelling and word recognition.

Fill in the Blanks: In this activity, students fill in missing words in sentences. This helps with grammar and context understanding.

Quiz: The quiz tests students' comprehension of the lesson's material. It's a good way to assess their understanding and retention.

Grammar Activities: These activities focus on specific grammar points covered in the lesson. They help students apply what they've learned in a structured way.

Matching Game: In the Matching Game, students are challenged to connect related items. This activity enhances their understanding and encourages deeper reflection on the lessons presented in the biblical text.
