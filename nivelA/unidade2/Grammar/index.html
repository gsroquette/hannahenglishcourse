The Verb "To Be" - Noah's Ark

A

Look at these sentences from the story of Noah's Ark:

  • I am Noah.
  • God is good.
  • The ark is big.
  • We are safe in the ark.
  • The animals are happy.

B

The verb "to be" is used to describe who or what something is, or to describe a state or condition.

am = I

is = he, she, it

are = you, we, they

  • I am happy. (I)
  • He is strong. (he)
  • They are animals. (they)

C

Now complete these sentences from the story:

1. Noah ___ building the ark. (is)

2. The animals ___ inside the ark. (are)

3. I ___ happy to help Noah. (am)

4. The ark ___ strong. (is)

5. They ___ a family. (are)

D

Write the correct form of the verb "to be":

1. God ___ good.

2. The ark ___ big.

3. We ___ safe.

4. You ___ strong.

5. They ___ happy.

E

Questions and Answers:

1. Who is building the ark?

- Noah is building the ark.

2. How many animals are in the ark?

- Two of each kind are in the ark.

3. What is God?

- God is good.
