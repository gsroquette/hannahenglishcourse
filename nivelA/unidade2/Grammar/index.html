The Verb "To Be" - Study with Noah's Ark

1. Introduction to the Verb "To Be"

The verb "to be" is one of the most important verbs in English. It means "to exist" or "to be in a state." Depending on the subject, the verb "to be" changes its form. Let's learn the forms of the verb "to be" in the present tense.

Subject Verb "To Be" Example
I am I am happy.
You are You are strong.
He/She/It is He is brave.
We are We are safe.
You (plural) are You are a family.
They are They are animals.

2. Examples from the Story of Noah

Let's see how the verb "to be" is used in the story of Noah.

  • I am Noah.
  • God is good.
  • The ark is big.
  • We are safe in the ark.
  • The animals are happy.
  • You are strong, Noah.
